Arduino-Based Smoke Detector with Early Warning System and Web-Based Monitoring

Project Overview

In the realm of safety and innovation, I conceptualized and developed an Arduino-based smoke detector that goes beyond traditional boundaries. This cutting-edge project combines hardware and software expertise to create a robust early warning system. The smoke detector is equipped with real-time smoke detection capabilities, triggering timely SMS alerts for prompt action.

To add an extra layer of convenience, I integrated a web-based monitoring interface. This allows users to remotely monitor the status of the smoke detector in real-time, ensuring peace of mind and efficient management. Through a harmonious fusion of hardware and web technologies, this project showcases my ability to create holistic solutions that prioritize safety and offer seamless user experiences.
