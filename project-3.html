Student Activity and Exam Tracker: Cross-Platform Mobile App for Recording and Managing Lacking Activities and Exams

Project Overview

Leveraging Xamarin and SQLite, this mobile app is tailored to assist teachers in monitoring student needs. The app simplifies the process of tracking and managing areas where students require attention. By utilizing SQLite's database capabilities, teachers can efficiently record and access essential information, facilitating better communication and support within the educational setting.
